# 🏛 ArchAgent — Your AI Architect on TON

**ArchAgent** is an intelligent, Telegram-native AI assistant designed for architecture, interior design, and real estate visualization. It combines Large Language Models (LLMs), Image Generation AI, and the **TON Blockchain** into a seamless architectural workflow.

Built for the **TON AI Agent Hackathon 2026** (User-Facing AI Agents Track).

---

## 🌟 Vision
Most AI image bots are simple, single-purpose tools. **ArchAgent** is a true **Autonomous Agent**. It understands the user’s intent and independently decides whether to:
- Provide professional architectural advice.
- Redesign an uploaded image (Image-to-Image).
- Generate a new architectural concept (Text-to-Image).
- Guide the user through design parameters (Styles, Weather, Lighting).

ArchAgent introduces a **TON-powered credit economy**, solving the challenge of sustainable monetization for high-compute AI infrastructure.

## ✨ Core Features

* 🤖 **Autonomous AI Architect:** Acts as a professional consultant, discussing materials (concrete, wood, glass), suggesting improvements, and answering design questions.
* 📸 **AI Image Redesign:** Upload a photo of a room, house, or building facade. ArchAgent preserves the original geometry using Structure-Control AI and redesigns it based on your instructions.
* 🎨 **Concept Ideation:** Generate high-fidelity architectural visualizations from natural language prompts (e.g., *"Design a modern desert villa with large glass windows"*).
* 🏙 **Environmental Controls:** Simulate professional visualization workflows:
    - **Styles:** Modern, Classic, Minimal, Luxury, Arabic.
    - **Time of Day:** Day, Night, Sunset.
    - **Weather:** Clear, Rain, Snow.
* 💎 **TON Credit Economy:** A seamless **Telegram Mini App (TMA)** connected via **TON Connect**. 
    - **Starter Pack:** 0.5 TON (150 Credits)
    - **Pro Pack:** 1 TON (400 Credits)
* 🌍 **Global Localization:** Automatically supports **English, Arabic, Persian, and Russian**.
* 👥 **Growth Engine:** Built-in **Referral System** (+10 Credits) to ensure organic viral growth.

## 🛠 Tech Stack

- **Backend:** Python 3 (FastAPI & python-telegram-bot v20+)
- **AI Engines:** OpenAI (GPT-4 reasoning), Stability AI (Image Synthesis & ControlNet)
- **Frontend:** HTML5, JS, Telegram WebApp SDK, `@tonconnect/ui`
- **Database:** SQLite3
- **Deployment:** Render (Keep-Alive via UptimeRobot)
